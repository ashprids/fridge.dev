world archive | minecraft

an archive of the minecraft worlds i've worked on throughout the past couple of years, from builds and survival to modpacks and multiplayer servers

All worlds are available for download as .zip files on Google Drive.
mc.fridge.dev mc.fridge.dev was a Minecraft server I ran between 18th Jan and 24th Feb 2026. This is the last backup before it discontinued.
Raspberry Flavoured A modpack based on rebalancing vanilla survival. This was my unfinished playthrough of the modpack.
OceanBlock Start on an island in the middle of nowhere and grow via automation. Kitchen sink modpack with progress up to Draconic Evolution.
fridgePack My personal progress on my own terribly and lazily made modpack with two friends, and another world by myself.
FTB Stoneblock Start in a world surrounded by stone. Not a lot of progress on this world, but I spent a lot of time on chickens.
SkyFactory 3 A great expansion on the Skyblock idea. This world is still occasionally played by me so this may be out of date.
